Blood collection centre

Our blood collection center provides safe, efficient, and patient-focused services tailored to the needs of cancer patients. With advanced equipment, highly trained staff, and strict hygiene protocols, we ensure accurate sample collection and processing.

The facility offers a wide range of diagnostic blood tests, blood donation services, and seamless integration with the hospital’s oncology department for transfusions and care coordination. Designed for comfort and accessibility, we prioritize patient well-being while supporting their treatment journey.

Pharmacy

Our oncology pharmacy, located within the hospital premises, provides comprehensive medication support for cancer patients, ensuring convenient and timely access to specialized care. Offering chemotherapy drugs, targeted therapies, and supportive medications, we prioritize precision and patient safety.

Our expert pharmacists provide personalized counseling, prepare medications under sterile conditions, and ensure proper storage. Integrated with the oncology department, the pharmacy delivers treatments efficiently, including nutritional supplements and pain management, all conveniently located within the hospital for easy access.

Day care services

Day Care Service offers safe administration of chemotherapy, immunotherapy, and other infusions, with expert supervision in a relaxing setting featuring recliner chairs and private cubicles. Our team of oncologists, nurses, and support staff provides compassionate care and closely monitors patients during treatments.

With flexible scheduling and same-day discharge options, we minimize hospital stays and waiting times, making it easier for patients to receive high-quality care with minimal disruption to their daily lives.

Hereditary Cancer screening

Our hereditary cancer screening center offers advanced genetic testing and counseling to identify and manage inherited cancer risks. We provide personalized risk assessments, preventive care planning, and expert guidance to help patients and families understand and address their cancer risk.

With state-of-the-art genomic tools and seamless integration with the hospital’s oncology team, our center ensures early detection, tailored interventions, and comprehensive support, empowering proactive health management in a convenient, accessible location.

Genetic counselling

Our genetic counseling services provide expert guidance to help patients understand hereditary cancer risks and make informed health decisions. Through personalized risk assessments based on family history and genetics, we offer counseling on genetic tests, their implications, and their role in cancer prevention and treatment.

We provide clear interpretations of test results, along with preventive strategies like early detection and lifestyle recommendations to reduce cancer risk. Our services also support family members in understanding genetic risks and testing options, all in a confidential and compassionate environment.

Empowering Your Journey with Pain and Palliative Care for Improved Comfort and Quality of Life

Pain and palliative care focuses on managing symptoms and providing relief to cancer patients, addressing both physical pain and emotional distress. This care ensures that patients maintain the best quality of life during their treatment journey.

By combining medical treatments, psychological support, and other therapies, pain and palliative care reduces suffering and helps patients cope with challenges. This holistic approach provides comfort and emotional support for both patients and their families.

Pain And Palliative Care at Noble

  • Patient Education and Support: HCG educates patients and families on pain management and available support services, empowering them to manage symptoms effectively at home.

  • Multidisciplinary Care Approach: HCG’s team of doctors, nurses, and psychologists collaborate to create personalized pain and palliative care plans tailored to each patient’s needs.

  • Pain Relief Solutions: HCG’s palliative care clinic utilizes medications, nerve blocks, and non-pharmacological therapies like acupuncture and relaxation to manage pain.

  • Quality of Life Enhancement: HCG’s care focuses on managing symptoms like pain and fatigue, offering emotional support, and assisting with advance care planning.

Medicines Used for Pain Relief in Palliative Care

1. Opioid medicines Opioids are used for moderate-to-severe pain in cancer patients during palliative care. They work by blocking pain signals in the brain and spinal cord. Available in pills, patches, injections, or liquids, opioids are customized based on the patient’s needs. When combined with non-opioid pain medicines, they offer better pain relief. Although opioids can be addictive, proper management and education ensure they improve quality of life under medical supervision.

2. Helper medicines Helper drugs, or adjuvants, complement opioid pain management in cancer care. These include antidepressants, anticonvulsants, and muscle relaxants, which address symptoms like anxiety, muscle spasms, and mood changes. By reducing opioid reliance and improving pain control, helper drugs enhance overall quality of life and provide comprehensive symptom relief.
